8 Things You Didnt Know About Javeria Saud

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

➡ Javeria Saud Biography

Javeria Saud is a Pakistani star whose skills are not confined to just acting, yet she is a great singer and host as well. She is popularly known for appearing as protagonist Jameela in the serial Yeh Zindagi Hai, aired on GEO TV. A very few people know that she is the wife of the veteran actor Saud who has also worked in the Pakistani industry. However, the couple is doing a lot as they both have established themselves in the field of showbiz. Today, we are bringing you some unknown things you probably did not know about.

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Javeria Saud

1- Known for

Javeria Saud is known for playing the role of Jameela in the top-ranked serial “Yeh Zindagi Hai” aired on Geo TV.

2- Multi-talented Star

Javeria Saud is a multi-talented actress as she flaunts her incredible skills. A very few people know that she is a singer, host, and producer as well.

3- Owns a Production House

In 2006, Javeria Saud owns a production house titled “JJS Productions” in the Pakistani entertainment industry and she runs alongside her husband Saud.

4- Her Comeback

A very few people know that the actress Javeria Saud took a gap from acting in the year 2001 and made comeback in 2006.

5- Co-production Project

In 2016, Javeria Saud signed a project with Babar Javed with whom she co-produced the serial “Khuda Aur Muhabbat”.

6- How she joined the showbiz

You will be amazed to know that Javeria Saud joined the showbiz as a Naat Reciter. And since then, she never looked back.

7- As a Host

Apart from acting and singing, Javeria Saud also hosted a Morning Show “Satrangi” since the year 2015.

8- Acting Debut

In 1995, the actress made her acting debut through a serial “Manzilen” and since then, she appeared in many popular dramas.
