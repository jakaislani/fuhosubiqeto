Jill Cunniff (born August 17, 1966) is an American musician and member of the band Luscious Jackson. Cunniff was born in New York City and attended college in San Francisco. After returning to New York in 1991, she formed Luscious Jackson with friend Gabby Glaser. Cunniff plays bass, sings lead vocals, and has been the chief songwriter for band.Cunniff joined with bandmate Vivian Trimble under the name Kostars and recorded an album released in 1996. In 2002, she formed a house music group named the Cooler Kids. Cunniff released a solo album, City Beach, in early 2007. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
