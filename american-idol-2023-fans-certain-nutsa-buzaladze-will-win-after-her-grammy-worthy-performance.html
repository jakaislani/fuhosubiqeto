'American Idol' 2023 fans certain Nutsa Buzaladze will win after her 'grammy-worthy' performance

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, June 4, 2024

TBILISI, GEORGIA: We are into Hollywood week! 'American Idol' Season 21 auditions have came to an end and the heat has been turned up. On April 2, episode the artists have to prove their metal to the judges Katy Perry, Luke Bryan, and Lionel Richie and fans can't wait to see who survives to reach the next level.

Hollywood week kicked off with show woman Nutsa Buzaladaze blowing the judge's and viewers' minds with her incredible performance. Nutsa set the stage on fire and fans believe she was holding back in her audition rounds. The fans of the ABC show have already predicted the winner and labeled Nutsa a "superstar."

Nutsa Buzaladze gets a standing ovation from judges and other participants 

Nutsa kicks off Season 21's Hollywood Week with a powerful rendition of Celine Dion's 'I Surrender', and leaves everyone speechless with her performance. Nutsa's rich voice and impressive stage presence not only left the 'American Idol' judges in awe but also left her fellow competitors stunned. Throughout Nutsa's performance, the trio of judges wears shocked expressions and Lionel can't stop saying "Wow." Katy's wide-eyed look didn't go unnoticed and by the end of Nutsa's song, everyone was on their feet.

The talented artist received a standing ovation from all three judges as well as her fellow competitors, which is an achievement. Luke even said, "I got chills everywhere," and Katy added, "We have really got some of the best talent this season." 

'American Idol' fans root for 'superstar' Nutsa Buzaladze

The fans of the show agree with the judges as they also think that Nutsa's performance was chill worthy. Many have expressed on the internet that Nutsa seemed to be holding back during her audition and that she is a born performer. Moreover, fans have labeled the young singer a superstar as one said, "She has a beautiful singing voice, she is a superstar." Another one said, "She has every quality to be a real star on the stage. She is amazing. Good luck Nutsa!" One more commented, "Wow, what an amazing way to start Hollywood week. She really set that bar high for everyone!!" Another added, "Easily one of the BEST Hollywood Week vocals in the 21-season history of @americanidol . This performance was Grammy-worthy @nutsabuza . Wow!"

Many fans have predicted that Nutsa can win the whole show after watching her performance as one fan commented, "I honestly can't believe what I just witnessed, this girl is ready to win.
