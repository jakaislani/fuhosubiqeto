Early Life and Education

Ryan Shazier was born on September 6, 1992, in Lauderdale Lakes, Florida, to parents Vernon and Shawn Shazier. He attended Plantation High School, where he played both football and track and field.

Shazier continued his athletic career at The Ohio State University, where he played football from 2011 to 2013. He declared for the NFL draft after his junior year and was selected as the 15th overall pick by the Pittsburgh Steelers in 2014.

Career

Personal Life

Illness and Death
