Josh Allen: Career stats, height, weight, family, college, teams, salary, net worth

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Born on May 21st, 1996, in Firebaugh, California, Josh Allen’s journey to the pinnacle of professional football is a testament to unwavering passion, relentless pursuit of excellence, and an arm that can launch rockets.

From the unforgiving terrain of Wyoming to the bright lights of the NFL, Allen has transformed himself into one of the league’s most feared and admired quarterbacks.

Professional Career Stats:

Allen’s career, though young, is already marked by impressive statistics and accolades:

Pro Bowl appearances: 2 (2020, 2023)

AFC Offensive Player of the Month: 1 (December 2020)

Completion Percentage: 65.6%

Passing Yards: 18,299

Passing Touchdowns: 142

Rushing Yards: 2,711

Rushing Touchdowns: 24

Height and Weight:

Standing at 6 feet 5 inches and weighing 237 pounds, Allen possesses the ideal physique for a modern quarterback. His imposing stature allows him to see over the offensive line and make precise throws, while his athleticism grants him the ability to escape pressure and extend plays with his legs.

College, Draft, and Teams Played:

After graduating from Reedley College in California, Allen transferred to the University of Wyoming, where he quickly became a star quarterback. Despite facing initial draft skepticism due to his completion percentage, Allen’s raw talent and potential were undeniable. He was selected by the Buffalo Bills with the seventh overall pick in the 2018 NFL Draft.

Jersey Number:

Allen proudly wears the number 17 jersey, a number synonymous with his leadership, athleticism, and powerful arm.

Super Bowl Rings:

While Allen has yet to reach the ultimate goal of a Super Bowl championship, his ambition and leadership continually fuel the Bills’ Super Bowl aspirations.

Religion and Ethnicity:

Allen is a devout Christian and has openly spoken about his faith’s impact on his life and career. He proudly identifies with his mixed-race heritage, inspiring others to embrace diversity and inclusion.

Parents and Siblings:

Allen maintains a close relationship with his family, who have been his unwavering support system throughout his journey. His parents, Joel and LaVonne Allen, instilled in him the values of hard work, dedication, and humility. He also enjoys a strong bond with his siblings, Jason and Nicala.

Wife or Girlfriend:

Allen is currently in a committed relationship with Brittany Williams. The couple enjoys a low-key life and remains focused on their respective careers.

Weekly and Annual Salary:

Allen’s talent and dedication are reflected in his lucrative contract, securing a weekly salary of $261,017 and an annual salary of $13,650,900.

Net Worth:

Estimates place Allen’s net worth between $20 million and $30 million. This financial success reflects his achievements as a professional athlete and his commitment to excellence both on and off the field.
