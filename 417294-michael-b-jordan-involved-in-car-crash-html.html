Michael B. Jordan Involved in Car Crash at 36 Known Details &amp; Photos

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 2, 2024

Michael B. Jordan | Source: Getty Images

Michael B. Jordan's Ferrari crashed into a parked car along Sunset Boulevard in Hollywood last weekend. Law enforcement raced to the scene and saw the luxury car missing one wheel.

Michael B. Jordan's Saturday night ended in a horrific accident along Sunset Boulevard in Hollywood. The actor was driving his car when it hit a parked Kia sedan on the side of the road.

The "Creed" actor had a male companion onboard when the accident happened. Both passengers were able to quickly exit the smashed vehicle after the crash.

The incident damaged both cars heavily. The front wheel in the passenger side of Jordan's blue Ferrari smashed into the Kia's back wheel, causing materials from both vehicles to bend and break. Jordan's wheel was eventually found away from the car.

The Los Angeles Police Department did not do a field sobriety test on Jordan, as the actor seemed normal at the time of the crash. When he was asked, "What happened?" the "Black Panther" actor did not respond.

The accident happened 30 minutes before midnight in a busy section of the famous road. No injuries were reported in the crash and no arrests were made in connection to the collision. Jordan was only asked to file a police report online.

The actor has long been a Ferrari enthusiast, recently collaborating with the brand for a video featuring his passion for race cars.
