El Paso, Tx, the Borderline Between the USA &amp; Mexico!

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 2, 2024

Tex Mex Motors on Netflix is mostly filmed in El Paso, Celeste ( both in Tx), and a few locations in Mexico. For those who are unaware, El Paso is the borderline between the United States and Mexico.

Tex Mex Motors on Netflix features some of the top car gurus in the United States who have joined forces to make their latest effort viable. Sales soar as they import vintage automobiles and restore them to their former splendor, but along the road, challenges must be overcome while having each other’s backs.

The team is shown searching for the vehicles, restoring them, and then looking for purchasers to sell them to in an effort to make a profit of at least $250,000 within four months. It’s a fairly conventional makeover reality program.

On the other hand, the location of the series also serves as an attraction for the viewers. As a result, many people have been wanting to know where it was filmed. Well, we’ve got you covered.

Filming Location: Tex Mex Motors Is Mostly Filmed in El Paso, Tx, the Borderline Between Mexico & the United States!

Tex Mex Motors on Netflix is actually filmed in 2 countries. While their makeover store is located in El Paso, Tx, where they do all the restoration and selling, the team goes across the borderline of Mexico to select the old unused cars they can restore and sell at a good profit.

Tex Mex Motors is mostly filmed in El Paso, Tx, and a few locations in Mexico.
Image Source: MT

According to The Cinemaholic, the first season of the reality show appears to have begun in early April 2022 and ended four months later, in August of the same year. Well, now let’s discuss the filming location in detail.

El Paso, Texas

The experts transport the gathered vintage cars to Texas, primarily to El Paso. It also goes by the name Sun City, and it’s where several crucial scenes from Tex Mex Motors were filmed. It is a borderline between the United States and Mexico.

The twenty-second-largest city in the US, El Paso, is located on the Rio Grande, on the other side of the border from Ciudad Juarez. It has a history of hosting filming locations, and you may undoubtedly be well aware that The Bridge and the second season of Breaking Bad were both filmed there.

Celeste, Texas

The video crew also establishes a base of operations at Zachary Customs, located at 8221 Co Road 1084 in Celeste, as the experts transform the cars in the auto restoration service facility with meticulous attention to detail. According to reports, they were also seen filming a couple more episodes of the show in various areas of the West Texas region.

Few scenes are filmed in Celeste, Texas as well.
Image Source: Texas Escapes

Mexico

In order to find abandoned automobiles that they can magically fix, the team of experts first travels all the way to Mexico. The distinctive Mexican landscape can be seen in the background, which gives the visuals for Tex Mex Motors additional depth. Mexico, sometimes known as the United Mexican States, is recognized for having a huge network of roads, as evidenced by the numerous multi-lane expressways that can be seen in various sequences of the Netflix series.

Furthermore, because there are numerous out shots of Mexico throughout the show, it is very probable that you will notice some popular monuments in the background, such as the Plaza de la Constitución, the Chapultepec Castle, the National Museum of Anthropology, and the Plaza de las Tres Culturas.

Juarez, Mexico

Additionally, Tex Mex Motors makes numerous pit stops in Juarez. The most populous city in the Mexican State of Chihuahua is Juarez, which was formerly known as El Paso del Norte until 1888. Given its dense population, it makes likely that the crew would have a higher likelihood of discovering abandoned cars there.
