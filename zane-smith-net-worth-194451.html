About

Rose to fame in 2017 portraying Cathy in the Amazon Original film An American Girl Story - Ivy & Julie 1976: A Happy Balance. She also brought to life Rain Eldenberg in the film Alex of Venice. 

Before Fame

She began gymnastics at the age of four and by nine was studying at The Superstars Dance Academy. She would then go on to join Emerson Community Charter School's Art & Media Academy. 

Trivia

She has guest starred in a number of popular television shows like Days of Our Lives and Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn. 

Family Life

She has one brother named Zander. 

Associated With

In An American Girl Story - Ivy & Julie 1976: A Happy Balance, she starred opposite Nina Lu and Hannah Nordberg.
