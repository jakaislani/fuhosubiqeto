Fortnite &quot;Dig At the Top of&quot; Encrypted Cipher Quest Guide

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Here's a comprehensive guide for completing the Encrypted Cipher Quest "Dig At the Top of" in Fortnite.

First Published March 2, 2023, 15:06

Fortnite Cipher quests are well underway, and as speculated, players are having a hard time completing the encrypted quests. The latest encrypted Cipher quest is 19.11.2.20.22.5.20.8.4. 22.13.1.4.19, which translates into Dig at the top of [Message Encrypted].

Although the quest is self-explanatory, the location where players must dig is still unknown to many. Therefore one may have trouble finishing this quest, which is why we have compiled this guide for completing the Dig at the top of... Fortnite challenge.

How to Complete Encrypted Cipher Quest "Dig At the Top of" in Fortnite

Before moving forward, this quest only unlocks when you complete the first three encrypted challenges of the Fortnite Cipher event. The fourth quest, titled "19.11.2.20.22.5.20.8.4. 22.13.1.4.19," requires you to Dig at the top of Shattered Slabs. We've marked the exact location in the above image for your convenience.

After landing at the location, climb to the top of Shattered Slabs and then proceed to the location shown in the below image. Here you will find a glowing rock near a chest. At this point, all you need to do is hit the stone with your harvesting tool by pressing the primary fire button.

Soon after it, you'll notice that the rock has now turned into an arcade machine, which means 19.11.2.20.22.5.20.8.4. 22.13.1.4.19 aKa Dig at the top of... Fortnite encrypted challenge has been marked as completed.

Upon completion, players will be rewarded with 10,000 XP and other glorious rewards that we've already talked about in another guide.

We want to thank the YouTube channel Fortnite Events for the complete walkthrough of the Dig at the top of the... challenge. Please consider subscribing to their channel and watching the video below for more details.

So there you have it. That's everything you need to know about completing stage four of the Fortnite encrypted Cipher quest.
